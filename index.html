Frequently Asked Questions

Do you transport Dangerous Goods?
Yes, we are fully certified and insured to transport various classes of dangerous goods. Our drivers are specially trained, and our equipment meets all regulatory standards.
What areas do you service?
We provide services across New South Wales, South Australia, Victoria, and Queensland. We handle both intrastate local deliveries and long-haul interstate routes.
Do you offer heavy machinery transport?
Absolutely. Our heavy haulage division operates specialized low loader floats capable of moving oversized and overweight machinery safely and efficiently.
Is my freight insured during transit?
Yes, we carry comprehensive marine transit insurance. You can also review our dedicated "Insurance" page for full coverage details and policy limits.
